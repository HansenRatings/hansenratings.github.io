Glossary of Terms

 

Team Metrics

AdjEff Opponent-Adjusted Efficiency A team s projected margin of victory against an average opponent on a neutral field

AdjO Opponent-Adjusted Offensive Efficiency A team s projected points for against an average team on a neutral field

AdjD - Opponent-Adjusted Defensive Efficiency A team s projected points allowed against an average team on a neutral field

AdjHFA Opponent-Adjusted Home-Field Advantage The expected positive/negative difference in team performance from a neutral field to home/away

Typical Top 25 Team A hypothetical team with an AdjEff rating of +30

Elite Team A hypothetical team with an AdjEff rating of +36

Good Team A hypothetical team with an AdjEff rating of +18

Average Team A hypothetical team with an AdjEff rating of 0

MOV+ Margin of Victory Plus How much more or less you ve outscored your opponents than a typical Top 25 team would be expected to

SOR Strength of Record The likelihood a typical Top 25 team would have a W-L record at least as good against the same opponents

E+ Elite Games The percentage of games where a team performed at an elite level or better

G+ Good Games The percentage of games where a team performed at a good level or better

A+ Average Games The percentage of games where a team performed at an average level or better

BA Below Average Games The percentage of games where a team performed at a below average level

EAW Elite Adjusted Wins How many more/fewer games the team has won than an elite team would be expected to win

GAW Good Adjusted Wins How many more/fewer games the team has won than a good team would be expected to win

AAW Average Adjusted Wins How many more/fewer games the team has won than an average team would be expected to win

Pool A The pool of playoff bids available to conferences champions

Pool B The pool of playoff bids available to independent teams or teams in a conference that does not have an automatic bid (every DIII football conference currently has an automatic bid, an not enough teams are independent to qualify for any Pool B bids)

Pool C The pool of playoff bids available for teams who did not receive a Pool A or Pool B bid

SOS Strength of Schedule The average expected win probability of a team s opponents against an average team

SOS+ Strength of Schedule Plus The average expected win probability of a team s opponents against a typical Top 25 team

NCSOS Non-Conference Strength of Schedule The average expected win probability of a team s non-conference opponents against an average team

 

Player Metrics

TAY/P Total Adjusted Yards per Play An efficiency metric that gives boosts for positive plays and penalties for negative plays:

pTAYP Playmaker Total Adjusted Yards per Play For defensive players, the average yards prevented due to the player s box score statistics

dTAYP Defender Total Adjusted Yards per Play For defenders, the average yards prevented/allowed due to their non-box score contributions to the overall team defensive efficiency

TAY/P+ Total Adjusted Yards per Play Plus Opponent-adjusted total adjusted yards per play

HVC Havoc Plays Tackles for loss, pass breakups, interceptions, forced fumbles, fumble recoveries, and blocked kicks/punts

YARP Yards Above Replacement Player How many yards an offensive player added/a defensive player prevented to his unit than a replacement player would have accounted for

Replacement Player A hypothetical player who has the average production and efficiency of all non-starters at his position
